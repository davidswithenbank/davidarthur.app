HIPAA & Privacy FAQ
Does DuoVox store patient audio or PHI?
In Private Mode, DuoVox does not store audio, transcripts, or metadata. Everything is processed in real time and discarded immediately.

Is DuoVox HIPAA compliant?
DuoVox does not claim HIPAA certification and does not sign Business Associate Agreements (BAAs).
Organizations determine whether using DuoVox fits their compliance requirements.

Can DuoVox be used in healthcare settings?
Yes. Many healthcare professionals use DuoVox in Private Mode because it does not store any audio or transcripts.
However, each organization must evaluate its own compliance obligations.

Does DuoVox send data to third‑party services?
In Private Mode, no.
In Standard Mode, audio may be processed through secure cloud services.

Does DuoVox use my data to train AI models?
No. DuoVox does not use Private Mode data for training.
Standard Mode data is never used for advertising or profiling.

Can DuoVox help my organization meet privacy requirements?
DuoVox provides privacy‑preserving tools, but compliance decisions remain with the organization.
