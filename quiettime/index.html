QuietTime

Simple screen time control that respects your family's privacy.

No accounts • No cloud • No tracking • No ads

Features

📅

Flexible Scheduling

Set separate block times for weekdays and weekends. Choose which days count as weekends to match your family's routine.

🔒

PIN-Protected Settings

A 6-digit PIN keeps children from changing settings or disabling the app. All settings require PIN entry.

🎨

Beautiful Block Screen

Five animated themes (Starry Night, Dreamy Clouds, Aquarium, Northern Lights, Fireflies) make the block screen friendly rather than frustrating.

📱

Allowed Apps

Choose which apps (phone, clock, etc.) remain accessible during quiet hours, displayed on the block screen for easy access.

⏰

Wind-Down Warnings

Optional notifications before quiet time starts, giving children time to finish what they're doing.

👥

Parent Controls

Grant extra time for special occasions, temporarily unlock the device, or lock it early when needed.

📊

Activity Log

Review when the device was locked, unlocked, and when overrides were used.

🛑

Emergency Access

Emergency call button always available on the block screen, plus an optional emergency contact number.

100% Private

Everything stays on your device. No accounts to create, no cloud servers, no analytics, no tracking, no ads. QuietTime never sends data anywhere. Read our Privacy Policy →

Windows Features

💻

Three Lock Levels

Gentle (dismissable warning for older teens), Standard (fullscreen overlay), or Strict (locks the Windows workstation).

⚡

Starts with Windows

Optional auto-start ensures quiet hours are always enforced, even after a restart.
