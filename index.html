Why DuoVox?

🎧

Dual-Lane Transcription

Two simultaneous AI streams capture both languages in a conversation independently, eliminating cross-talk errors and delivering accurate real-time text.

🌐

15+ Languages

From English and Russian to Japanese, Arabic, Hindi and many more. The full interface is localised in every supported language, not just the transcription.

⚡

Instant AI Translation

Translations appear within seconds of speech. Context-aware AI understands idioms, technical terms and natural conversation flow.

🔒

Offline Draft Mode

No internet? On-device speech recognition provides draft transcription and translation locally, so you never lose a word even without connectivity.

📱

Android & Windows

Use DuoVox on your phone for face-to-face conversations, or on your desktop for meetings, calls and media. Same experience, any device.

🛡

Privacy-First Design

Zero data retention. Audio is processed in real-time and never stored. Transcriptions exist only in device memory and are cleared when your session ends. No recordings, no logs, no data on our servers.

Accuracy Disclaimer

DuoVox uses AI-powered speech recognition and machine translation to assist communication. Like all transcription and translation tools, results may contain errors and should not be treated as a verbatim or certified record. Accuracy can be affected by background noise, accents, microphone quality, overlapping speech and domain-specific terminology. DuoVox is a communication aid and should not be relied upon for legal, medical, safety-critical, financial or official purposes. Always verify important information independently.
