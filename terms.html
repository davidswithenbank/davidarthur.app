1. Description of Service

DuoVox is a real-time voice transcription and translation application. The App captures audio, transcribes speech to text, and provides AI-powered translations between supported languages. The App is available on Android and Windows platforms.

2. Subscription Tiers

The App offers the following tiers:

  • Free: On-device transcription using local speech recognition. Offline translation can be unlocked temporarily by watching a short video.
  • Essential ($25/month): AI transcription and translation using our Economy AI engine. Includes 30 hours of AI usage per month. Additional usage beyond the included hours is available on a pay-as-you-go basis.
  • Premium ($65/month): High Quality AI transcription and translation for improved accuracy. Includes 40 hours of AI usage per month. Includes subtitle export (SRT and VTT formats) and file translation. Additional usage beyond the included hours is available on a pay-as-you-go basis.
  • Business ($250/month): All Premium features with unlimited usage (subject to a fair-use policy of 200 hours per month). Priority support. Designed for professional, legal, medical and media environments.

All paid tiers are available with annual billing at a discounted rate (equivalent to two months free). Subscriptions are billed through Google Play and are subject to Google Play's terms and refund policies.
