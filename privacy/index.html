Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

David Arthur Software ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data our products collect, how it is used, and your rights. It covers all our products including DuoVox and QuietTime.

Data Controller: David Arthur Software, United Kingdom
Contact: info@davidarthur.app

1. QuietTime

1.1 Data Collected

QuietTime operates entirely offline. It collects and stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your PIN and recovery password (encrypted)
  • Schedule settings (weekday/weekend times, weekend day selections)
  • Theme and lock level preferences
  • Allowed app selections
  • Activity log (block start/end, overrides, extensions, settings changes)

1.2 Data NOT Collected

QuietTime does not:

  • Transmit any data to any server
  • Require an account or login
  • Use analytics, tracking, or telemetry
  • Display advertisements
  • Collect personal information
  • Access contacts, messages, photos, or location

1.3 Permissions (Android)

QuietTime requires the following Android permissions to function:

  • Display over other apps — To show the block screen on top of other applications
  • Accessibility service — To prevent the block screen from being dismissed by children

These permissions are used solely for screen time control and are not used to monitor, record, or transmit any user activity.

2. DuoVox

2.1 Data Collected

Free tier (offline): All audio processing happens on your device using local models. No data is sent to any server. No account is required.

Paid tiers (online): Audio is streamed in real time to third-party AI service providers for transcription and translation. This audio is processed in real time and is not stored on any server. Transcribed and translated text exists only in your device's memory during the session.

2.2 Service Infrastructure Data

For paid tiers, we maintain minimal infrastructure data to manage your subscription:

  • An anonymous device identifier linked to your subscription tier
  • Usage minutes (for tracking against your monthly allocation)
  • Device verification data (to prevent subscription sharing)
  • Ad grant status (Free tier only)

2.3 Data NOT Collected

DuoVox does not:

  • Store audio recordings on any server
  • Store transcriptions or translations on any server
  • Collect personal information unless you contact support
  • Track your location
  • Sell or share data with third parties for marketing

2.4 Third-Party Services

DuoVox uses the following third-party services:

  • AI processing providers — For real-time transcription and translation (paid tiers). Audio is streamed and processed in real time; it is not stored.
  • Google AdMob / Microsoft WebView2 — For displaying advertisements (Free tier only). These services may use cookies or device identifiers as described in their own privacy policies.
  • Google Play Billing / Microsoft Store — For subscription management.

3. Our Websites

Our websites (davidarthur.app and duovox.net) are static sites hosted on GitHub Pages. They do not currently use cookies, analytics, or tracking scripts. Contact forms are processed by web3forms, which receives only the information you submit.

4. Payment Processing

Payments made through our website are processed by Paddle, who acts as the merchant of record. Paddle collects payment information directly — we do not see or store your payment card details. Paddle's privacy policy governs the handling of your payment data.

Payments made through app stores (Google Play, Microsoft Store) are governed by those platforms' respective privacy policies.

5. Your Rights (GDPR / UK GDPR)

If you are in the UK or European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure — Request deletion of your data
  • Portability — Request your data in a portable format
  • Objection — Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent — Where processing is based on consent (e.g., ad personalisation)

For QuietTime, all data is stored locally on your device — you can delete it at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling.

For DuoVox, contact support@duovox.net to exercise any of these rights.

Legal Basis for Processing

Where we process personal data, the legal basis is:

  • Contract performance — Processing audio for transcription/translation as part of the DuoVox service
  • Legitimate interests — Subscription management, fraud prevention, service improvement
  • Consent — Ad personalisation (Free tier, where applicable)

6. California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, request its deletion, and opt out of any sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

7. Children's Privacy

QuietTime is designed for parents to manage their children's screen time. The app does not collect any data from children.

DuoVox is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA/UK). If we become aware that we have collected data from a child, we will delete it promptly.

8. International Data Transfers

DuoVox paid tiers stream audio to AI providers whose servers may be located outside the UK/EEA. These transfers are protected by standard contractual clauses and the providers' data processing agreements. Audio is processed in real time and is not stored.

QuietTime does not transfer any data internationally as it operates entirely offline.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our products constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact
