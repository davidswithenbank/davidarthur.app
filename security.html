Security & Privacy at DuoVox

Your conversations deserve the highest level of protection. DuoVox is built with a privacy-first architecture that gives users control over their data.

Zero-Retention Private Mode

When Private Mode is enabled:

  • Audio is processed in real time and immediately discarded
  • No recordings or transcripts are stored on any server
  • No data is logged or retained by DuoVox
  • No information is used for analytics or model training

This mode is ideal for interpreters, healthcare professionals, legal teams, and anyone handling sensitive information.

Standard Mode (Cloud Processing)

When Private Mode is not enabled:

  • Audio is streamed to third-party AI services for real-time transcription and is not stored by DuoVox
  • Transcriptions and translations exist only in your device’s memory during the session
  • No data is sold or shared with advertisers
  • Third-party AI providers process data under their own privacy policies and do not use API data for model training under current policies

Data Handling Principles

DuoVox follows these core principles:

  • Minimal data collection — we collect only what is needed to provide the service
  • No unnecessary retention — data is discarded as soon as it is no longer needed
  • No sale of user data — your information is never sold to third parties
  • No advertising-based profiling — we do not build profiles of our users
  • Clear, transparent communication — we tell you exactly what happens with your data

Compliance Transparency

DuoVox is designed with strong privacy protections, but:

  • DuoVox does not claim certification under any regulatory framework (including HIPAA, FERPA, or CCPA)
  • DuoVox does not sign Business Associate Agreements (BAAs)
  • Organizations are responsible for determining whether DuoVox fits their compliance needs

This ensures clarity and avoids misleading claims. For common questions about using DuoVox in regulated settings, see our Privacy FAQ.

Security Practices

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS) for all communications
  • Secure infrastructure with industry-standard protections
  • No DuoVox servers store user audio, transcriptions, or personal data
  • Regular security reviews and updates

Your Control

You can:

  • Enable Private Mode at any time for zero-retention sessions
  • Clear all session data instantly with the Clear button
  • Use DuoVox offline where supported — no data leaves your device
  • Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data

For our full data handling policy, see the Privacy Policy. For questions about regulated environments, see the Privacy FAQ.
